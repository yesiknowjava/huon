Our Story

We’re proud to say that we’ve been transforming our clients’ businesses since 1989.

Starting in our founder’s garage, Huon IT has grown year by year maintaining offices in Sydney and Melbourne providing a broad range of end to end IT services to a diverse portfolio of clients.

Our team is a potent mix of technical experience and business acumen, enabling our renowned ‘shape to fit’ approach and solution for our clients.
