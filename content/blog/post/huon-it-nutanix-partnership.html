Huon IT Announces Nutanix Partnership

Huon IT welcomes Nutanix to our team of partners. Nutanix offers an innovative approach to IT infrastructure using a hyper-converged platform and solutions that are 100% software based. The solution makes infrastructure invisible, enabling IT to focus on the applications and services that power their business.
